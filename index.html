About

Dr. Andrew S. Rokito is a board-certified orthopedic surgeon with extensive clinical and academic experience in musculoskeletal medicine. He is affiliated with NYU Langone Health and has served as a treating physician, consultant, and medical expert for decades.

In addition to his clinical practice, Dr. Rokito provides independent medical evaluations and medical record reviews for legal, insurance, and administrative matters.

Services Offered

  • Independent Medical Examinations (IMEs / CMEs)
  • Medical record reviews
  • Written medical reports
  • Telephonic or virtual medical conferences, when requested

Professional Background

Dr. Rokito is a long-standing member of the orthopedic faculty at NYU Langone Health and has published and lectured extensively in the field of orthopedics.
